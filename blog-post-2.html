Modern web design is evolving. We are seeing a resurgence of 90s aesthetics, characterized by raw HTML, pixelated graphics, and bright colors.

This style, often called "Neo-Brutalism" or "Y2K Aesthetic", is a reaction against the overly polished, minimalist "Corporate Memphis" style that has dominated the web for the last decade.


Key Features of Retro Design

  • Pixel fonts and icons
  • Vibrant, high-contrast colors
  • Visible borders and frames
  • Lack of smooth gradients and shadows

It's not just about nostalgia; it's about bringing personality and fun back to the web!
