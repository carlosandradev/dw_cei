Welcome to My First Post!

Hello standard world! This is my very first blog post on this retro-styled website.

I decided to build this site to bring back the nostalgia of the 90s web. Back then, things were simpler, pixelated, and full of charm.


Why Retro?

Modern web design is great, simplified, and minimal. But sometimes, it lacks personality. I wanted to create something that feels like a personal computer from 1995.


What to expect?

I will be posting updates about my projects, web development tips, and maybe some random thoughts about technology.


Thanks for visiting!
